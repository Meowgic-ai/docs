## Start integration

You can integrate your app in two ways:
- Automatic integration: If your Meowgic account is already connected to your SHOPLINE store, you can pick a target theme and let Meowgic handle installation and future updates automatically.
- Manual integration: Download your theme file from SHOPLINE and upload it to Meowgic. Meowgic AI applies the integration, and you then download the updated theme file and upload it back to SHOPLINE.

When you create or edit an app, Meowgic AI uses both your conversation context and app details to decide the best placement in your theme. For example, if you asked to place a countdown app in the product info area, it will only appear in product page templates.

## Tips

- If you run into issues while configuring your Meowgic app in SHOPLINE, or want further adjustments, describe what you need directly on the integration page. Meowgic AI can apply the changes automatically. You usually only need to return to the app editor when the app's admin settings or core behavior changes significantly.
- If your app covers product-related scenarios, such as color options, product customization, or gift features, the system integrates based on the requirements you defined during app creation. After integration, you can keep refining behavior with Meowgic AI, such as attaching custom parameters on add-to-cart or adjusting cart styles and interactions.
- You can also use Meowgic AI to design your theme.
- Explore more features and use cases as your store evolves.
