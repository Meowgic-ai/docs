## 1. Configure preset data

After you publish your app, click **Submit your app**.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/86e28fbc-a64b-4a1c-b4b8-e5d0841a42a4.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The published app sidebar with the Submit your app option highlighted."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

Meowgic asks whether you want to provide preset data.

If your app does not have an Admin area, click **Keep data empty**.

If your app has an Admin area and you want to include initial data, click **Provide preset data**.

<Info>
  **Preset data (Provide preset data)** is the initial data included with your app for other Meowgic users. When users get your app from the Meowgic App Store, they receive this data with it.
</Info>

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/40ad3993-7d77-4b96-a7c4-a2929481803f.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="A dialog asking whether the app provides preset data, with Keep data empty and Provide preset data buttons."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

After you click **Provide preset data**, choose how to provide it. Select one of the following options, or use a custom option to describe which data you can provide:

- **Auto-filter safe preset data**: Meowgic uses AI to review your current app data and selects only data that is safe to display publicly as preset data.
- **Use all current data**: Use all of your app's current data as preset data.
- **Do not provide preset data**: Do not provide any preset data for your app.
- **Other**: Add your own instructions to specify which data can be used as preset data.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/4a49e6f7-c771-4957-b9ec-c885b33dd1dd.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The preset data options: Auto-filter safe preset data, Use all current data, Do not provide preset data, and Other."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

Meowgic saves preset data based on your selection. Continue after you see a success message.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/afa9470b-27c3-4240-8450-cb77d01b0c46.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The Meowgic chat panel shows Auto-filter safe preset data selected and preset data processing in progress."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

## 2. Submit your app for review

After you configure preset data, click the app name in the upper-left corner to open its menu, then select **Submit your app**.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/99eaf8b8-0f90-4452-84d9-6ea975d77a73.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The app name menu button in the upper-left corner of the Meowgic editor."
  />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/70743443-edb9-48de-bea1-4fbb1724143d.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The Submit your app option in the app name menu."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

In the **Submit your app** window, complete the basic app information, App Store listing content, demo URL, pricing details, and contact information.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/0b997c9d-b20a-48d2-83fd-e32e92743864.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The Basic app information section of the Submit your app form, with app name, app icon, and category fields."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

When you finish, click **Submit** in the lower-right corner.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/3908f479-a8d4-4e93-9356-bfd7c46ebd65.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The Submit button in the lower-right corner of the completed Submit your app form."
  />
</Frame>

In the confirmation dialog, click **Submit** again to send your app for review.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/89931a68-426c-4178-9ada-0a92c1d2469e.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The confirmation dialog for submitting the app, with Later and Submit buttons."
  />
</Frame>

## 3. Check review status

After you submit your app, its status changes to **Pending Review** and the Meowgic team starts reviewing it. At this stage, click **Withdraw** to cancel the submission.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/da504e07-5393-4beb-9dd6-d975ec564cae.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The app submission list with the status set to Pending Review and a Withdraw button."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

When the status is **In Review**, the Meowgic team is reviewing your app.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/c03cab2a-a6dc-4153-b423-47a64c64415a.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The app submission list with the status set to In Review."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

After approval, the status changes to **Live**. Click **App detail** to view your app's listing in the Meowgic App Store.

To remove a live app, click **Remove**.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/e70e77cd-e458-449c-87c8-b9a666b974aa.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The approved app submission with the status set to Live and App detail and Remove buttons."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

If your app does not pass review, do not worry. Click **View reason** to see Meowgic's feedback. Make the requested updates, then submit your app for review again.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/44fe460a-df91-4332-8091-dca3ec857986.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The rejected app submission with a View reason button."
  />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260714/2559cd9b-0cb1-45ed-809a-0b80fc71fb69.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp"
    alt="The rejection-reason dialog for an app that did not pass review."
  />
</Frame>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is preset data?">
    Preset data is the initial data included with your app for other Meowgic users. When users get your app from the Meowgic App Store, they receive this data with it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does my app need preset data if it has no Admin area?">
    No. When you submit your app, click **Keep data empty**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How should I choose a preset data option?">
    We recommend **Auto-filter safe preset data** in most cases. Meowgic uses AI to select data that is safe to display publicly. Choose **Use all current data** only if all of your current data can be shared. Choose **Other** if you have specific requirements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What information do I need to submit?">
    Complete the basic app information, App Store listing content, demo URL, pricing details, and contact information. This includes your app name, icon, category, introduction, details, features, video URL, screenshots, support email, and app submission email.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw a submission?">
    Yes. When the status is **Pending Review**, click **Withdraw** to cancel the submission.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What should I do if my app does not pass review?">
    Click **View reason** to see Meowgic's feedback. Make the requested updates, then submit your app for review again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I preview an approved app listing?">
    When the status changes to **Live**, click **App detail** to view its listing in the Meowgic App Store.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
