Meowgic Cloud is a full-stack cloud platform built for AI-powered e-commerce app creation. From the storefront experience your users interact with to the backend services running behind the scenes, Meowgic handles every part of your application automatically.

Simply describe what you want in Meowgic, and it generates both the frontend and backend for your app — ready to run instantly in the Cloud. No server setup, infrastructure management, or complex deployment workflows required.

With built-in hosting, database, authentication, storage, edge functions, and AI capabilities, Meowgic Cloud provides scalable compute and storage that automatically grow with your app and business.

## Why choose Meowgic Cloud

- **Scales as you grow**: From your first e-commerce app to large-scale production workloads, Meowgic Cloud scales seamlessly with your business and traffic growth.
- **Secure by default**: Enterprise-grade authentication, secure cloud infrastructure, fine-grained access controls, and modern security best practices help protect your apps, customer data, and business operations.
- **Cost-effective**: Manage multiple apps and projects in one workspace with flexible usage-based pricing. Meowgic Cloud is designed for extremely low operating costs, helping you launch, test, and scale your apps without expensive infrastructure overhead.

## Enabling Meowgic Cloud

By default, our AI agent automatically detects whether your app requires Cloud features and will proactively prompt you to enable them when needed. You can also manually enable Meowgic Cloud anytime by going to **App → Cloud → Enable Meowgic Cloud**.

## Region selection

Meowgic Cloud projects can be hosted in three different regions:

- Americas
- Europe
- Asia Pacific

Choosing a region closer to your users results in:

- Lower latency
- Faster database responses
- Region-based data hosting
- Better overall backend performance

<Note>
	After Cloud is enabled, you cannot change the selected region, and you
	cannot move existing projects between regions.
</Note>

## Lovable Cloud features

You can access the Cloud tab inside any Meowgic project by clicking the Cloud button next to Preview mode.

### Database

- **Generate schemas automatically**: describe the data your app needs in plain language, and Meowgic automatically creates the database tables and schema for you.
- **Manage without SQL**: view, edit, and organize records directly in the UI. Update values, inspect tables, and keep your app’s data in sync without writing queries.

### Storage

- **File handling without code**: upload, manage, and serve files such as product images, user uploads, documents, videos, and other static assets directly in Meowgic Cloud — no backend setup required.
- **Storage buckets**: files are securely stored in dedicated project buckets, with URLs and references ready to use in your app. 
- **Limits**: Supports files up to 1 GB.

### AI

- **Built-in AI capabilities**: Meowgic Cloud comes with built-in AI features, allowing you to quickly create smarter, more interactive, and more engaging app experiences without additional setup.
- Some examples of AI powered features include:
  - **AI summaries** – automatically condense long text into clear takeaways
  - **AI chatbot or agent** – build conversational helpers into your app
  - **Sentiment detection** – understand user feedback at scale
  - **Image style tool** — Transform images into different artistic styles
- You can monitor Meowgic AI usage and performance for your project from the AI overview.

### Usage

The **Usage** tab provides a visual overview of how your app is consuming Meowgic Cloud resources over a selected time period, including compute, storage, database, AI usage, and other cloud services.

The time period defaults to **Last 7 days**. You can switch to **Last day** from the dropdown to adjust the period shown. Cloud usage updates daily at 10 AM CET.

Usage is broken down across **resource categories**, displayed as a segmented bar and listed individually with a percentage:

- **Database server**: used when your app queries or updates data. Scalable data services are coming soon.
- **Database storage**: the disk space used by your database to store your app’s data (like users, orders, and settings).
- **Compute**: used when your app’s code runs.
- **Storage**: used to store files like images, videos, and uploads.
- **Live updates**: used to send real-time updates to users (for example, notifications or chat).
- **Network**: used when your app sends or receives data over the internet.

## Usage-based Cloud and AI pricing

Meowgic includes Cloud hosting and built-in AI capabilities across all pricing plans, including the Free plan. Cloud services use a flexible usage-based pricing model, meaning costs scale based on your app’s actual resource usage and are billed separately from your subscription plan.

From your app, go to Settings → Usage. Here, you can:

- View your workspace balance status
- Top up and add funds to your workspace balance (available for paid plans only)
- Access your invoice history and manage payment preferences
- Track total Cloud and AI usage costs across your workspace
- View detailed Cloud and AI usage costs for each project

The Cloud & AI balance usage screen updates as follows:

- Cloud usage updates daily at 10 AM CET
- AI usage updates continuously

### Adding funds (paid plans only)

Adding funds to your workspace balance for Meowgic Cloud and AI usage is available exclusively on paid subscription plans.

Usage is billed separately and not included in your subscription.

- **Cloud usage**: depends on activity (number of users and interactions) and data transfer (uploads/downloads).
- **AI usage**: costs depend on the AI model selected and the complexity of the task. More advanced models typically consume more resources and result in higher usage costs. To verify pricing details, please refer to the official sources linked in the supported AI models section of Meowgic AI.
