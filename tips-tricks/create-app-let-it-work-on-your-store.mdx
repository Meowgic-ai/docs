# 1. Create the app

First, go to the [Meowgic homepage](https://app.meowgic.ai/en).

  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260703/96daffc3-4fa4-4211-98e7-d7041a9d6008.png"
    alt="Meowgic homepage with the app prompt box and suggested app templates."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

Enter the app you want to create in the **Meowgic prompt box**, just like writing a prompt for an AI image generator.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260706/29fd2b7f-fa58-409a-95ea-46328744d12b.png"
    alt="User enters an app idea in the Meowgic homepage prompt box."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

For example: create a customizable FAQ section.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260706/8290d9a0-35f1-4d20-955b-8ff58675afbb.png"
    alt="Screenshot of the Custom FAQ Section editor with the Publish panel open, highlighting the publish URL and Publish button."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

After you finish entering the prompt and press Enter, Meowgic will analyze your requirements and ask follow-up questions. Choose or answer based on what you have in mind.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260706/2a1cba2c-839b-41dc-bf15-f4b78ae1b910.png"
    alt="Meowgic asks how FAQ content should be managed, with theme editor blocks selected."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

After the requirements plan is complete, Meowgic will generate a design checklist for you to confirm.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260706/b24b0971-b376-4c82-8766-d35391a4f79e.png"
    alt="Meowgic shows the planned FAQ section design and the Approved, Start building option."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

After you confirm, Meowgic will list the to-do items and start building your app.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260706/80216e04-fadd-46c5-8cab-16fb258a5b31.png"

</Frame>

<br />

When the build is complete, Meowgic will send you a message in the chat, and the app will appear in the preview area.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260706/b3bd3472-dd23-473c-81b0-7bfbea032f60.png"

<Frame>

# 2. Publish the app

After you finish testing the app, click **Publish** in the upper-right corner to publish it.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260706/b5e58b0b-7c2d-4a98-8897-a4d141b824ee.png"
    alt="Publish button in the top-right corner of the Meowgic preview workspace."
  />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260706/c0f27250-ffbb-4004-8dcc-9185ab15f77b.png"
    alt="Publish dialog with fields for the app icon, title, and optional description."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

1. Enter the app name.

2. Enter the app description. If you leave it blank, AI will generate one automatically.

After filling in the fields, click **Continue** at the bottom of the publish dialog.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260706/cbbd5db2-4aed-4cc2-bc4e-33d9a078aca2.png"
    alt="Publish form for entering the app name and optional description before continuing."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

1. Meowgic will automatically generate an app link for you. You can open this link in a browser to preview the app, and you can also customize the link prefix.

2. After you click the publish button, this app version will be officially published.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260706/d230a5c0-f2d4-4d87-8242-b0890739abed.png"
    alt="Publish dialog showing the generated app URL and the final Publish button."
  />
</Frame>

<br />

After the app is published, you can choose how to install it.

If your store is built on Shopify, SHOPLINE, or Shoplazza, choose the matching platform option in the published app panel to start the integration process.

Self-hosted site users can copy the app link and embed it into their own site.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.staticmeow.com/meowgic/static/images/20260706/d998a715-8938-4f10-8ae9-b4bcda8ffd7b.png"
    alt="Published app panel with the app link and store installation options."
  />
</Frame>

<Tip>
  If you only want to quickly check the result, open the app link first. When you are ready to install it in your store, continue with the theme integration.
</Tip>

<Note>
  If you update or modify the app after publishing it, publish it again. Otherwise, the integration or preview link will not use the latest version.
</Note>

<br />

# 3. Integrate your app into your store

After entering the integration page, first download the theme template you are currently using, then upload it to Meowgic.

<Warning>
  When downloading the theme template, choose the theme your store is currently using. Uploading the wrong theme file may make the integration result inconsistent with your live store.
</Warning>

Choose your store platform to find the download location:

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to publish the app again after editing it?">
    Yes. If you update the app after publishing, publish it again so the preview link and integration use the latest version.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I install the same Meowgic app on Shopify, SHOPLINE, and Shoplazza?">
    Yes, but each platform requires you to download and upload its own theme file.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
